ROSTED CHICKPEA SALAD

Author : Juan

Prep time : 5 mins

Cook time : 40 mins

Serves : 4 - 6

Description

This simple green salad is loaded up with zesty roasted chickpeas, cucumber, avocado, feta cheese, fresh herbs, and a tangy lemon vinaigrette.
